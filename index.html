Islamic Heritage Calculator

Note:
  • Keep in mind that, grand-sons or grand-daughters do not get inheritance as long as the sons or daughters of the deceased person is still alive, even though the father (son of the deceased person) of the grand-sons or grand-daughters has died, if there are other sons or daughters, then the grand-sons or grand-daughters will not inherit. This also applies to the case of grand-grand-sons or grand-grand-daughters. Or in other words there is no substitute heir in Islamic law.
  • It should also be noted that all other family members who are not listed below are not heirs of the deceased person.
