Note:
  • Keep in mind that, whoever is born to someone, then he does not get an inheritance as long as that someone is still alive, but if that someone has died then he gets an inheritance. Especially for the case of a son who dies before the deceased person dies, then his share is still counted, then his share is handed over to his heirs, so that it can be calculated again for the share that is intended for his heirs.
  • It should also be noted that all other family members who are not listed below are not heirs of the deceased person.
